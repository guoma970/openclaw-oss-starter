# Community Reply Helper

Draft short replies for community interaction around a public project.

## Use cases

- answer “what does this do?”
- reply to launch comments
- thank people for interest
- clarify roadmap questions
- respond to “how do I use this?”

## Workflow

1. Identify the comment intent.
2. Answer directly in one or two short paragraphs.
3. Add a link or next step when useful.
4. Keep the tone warm and grounded.

## Rules

- Be concise.
- Be factual.
- Do not sound defensive.
- Do not over-explain unless asked.
- When possible, point to GitHub, ClawHub, or the relevant skill.
